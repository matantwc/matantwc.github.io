Do you have the dexterity to become an expert Truck Loader? Use your magnetic lift pully to get the goods in the back of the pickup truck for transporting. Load up the trucks and send them on their way in 20 different challenging levels. Truck loader is a test of patience and mouse skills, requiring practice.

Heli Support is a physics based helicopter game. You must airlift objects and place them carefully to build a path for the tanks, or any other army vehicles. There are 12 levels, all with increasing difficulty. Keep an eye on your fuel meter too, as if you run out of fuel, your helicopter will crash and youâ€™ll fail the level. You can refuel by flying back to your base and landing gently. The controls for the ropes are a bit hard to master at first, but after some practice you'll soon become a pro at using them.

Can you deliver all the right packages to the right customers before the time runs out? Help your trolley delivery service deliver the goods on time in this quirky drive-and-delivery game! Guide the trolley on the road using your keyboard arrows, but don't overdo it! Speeding up too much will make it difficult to stop under the right shops, and you don't want to break any crates!

Ever played tic-tac-toe? If you have, then four square comes with a little more challenging goal. Make a square out of four blocks of your color. Each square grants you a number of points. Watch out for the adversary though 'cause he will be trying to do the same thing. Place you squares strategically so that you both block your opponent and complete your square at the same time. Think quick, act quicker!

Working on the ship yard has never been easy. Your massive magnetic crane will help, but not too much. Moving the cargo from the platform onto the boat, you'll have to carefully place the cargo to the best of your ability, clicking to activate the magnetic pull and releasing to let it go. Don't throw it too far or you'll miss the target!

Hit Logic is back with even more fiendish checker puzzles for you to solve! Get all the red pieces out of the board in the smallest amount of moves, and be careful not to dispose of the other colors! Can you solve all the puzzles or will you give up? Your logic skills will be put to the test in this difficult puzzle game!
